# liteeth Design Decisions

Per-platform notes for the `liteeth` design (Verilog top
`liteeth_udp_usp_gth_sgmii`) ‚Äî the LiteEth Ethernet stack (LiteX/Python)
configured with a UDP endpoint over the Xilinx UltraScale+ GTH-SerDes
SGMII PHY.

This is the most complex liteeth variant: UDP streaming protocol + a high-speed serial PHY whose Xilinx UltraScale+ GTH transceiver brings in the most logic, and the one that exercises the most OpenROAD corner cases. It is the sole liteeth design kept in HighTide as of 2026-05-12 ‚Äî the other five variants (`mac_axi_mii`, `mac_wb_mii`, `udp_raw_rgmii`, `udp_stream_rgmii`, `udp_stream_sgmii`) were removed when the suite was simplified.

| Verilog top                 | Protocol | PHY                          |
|---|---|---|
| `liteeth_udp_usp_gth_sgmii` | UDP      | Xilinx UltraScale+ GTH SGMII |

FakeRAM macros live at `designs/<platform>/liteeth/sram/{lef,lib}/`.

## FakeRAM regeneration (2026-05-13)

- **Generator**: `bsg_fakeram` (VLSIDA fork @ `asap7-area-calib-v2`), via `tools/regenerate_sram.sh liteeth <platform>`.
- **Cfg**: `designs/src/liteeth/dev/generated/fakeram_{asap7,nangate45,sky130hd}.cfg` ‚Äî 2 macros each, `1rw1r` (1 rw + 1 read-only), `no_wmask`.
- **Prior generator**: OpenFakeRAM (LEFs marked `# Generated by OpenFakeRAM`) on all three platforms.
- **Size delta**: asap7 macros grow ~5√ó (the prior OpenFakeRAM LEFs were unrealistically dense for 7nm); nangate45 / sky130hd within 1‚Äì2 % of CACTI's previous outputs. `macros.v` at `designs/src/liteeth/macros.v` already uses bsg_fakeram's `rw0_*` / `r0_*` pin names, so no RTL update was needed.

## Active workarounds

- **ODB-1200** in CTS-time `repair_timing` affects **asap7**.  Fixed via `SKIP_CTS_REPAIR_TIMING = 1` (skip the entire repair pass; route's own hold-repair still runs).  See [HighTide#75](https://github.com/VLSIDA/HighTide/issues/75).

### Decisions
- **2026-04-30 `c8d96617`**: hit ODB-1200 in CTS repair_timing once the OpenROAD pin moved from `5f1bd87f` to `578be38a` (HighTide #103). Same bug class that previously affected `liteeth_udp_stream_sgmii` (PR #76). Added `SKIP_CTS_REPAIR_TIMING=1`.
