NVDLA

Verilog asap7
asap7partition_a asap7partition_m asap7partition_o

NVDLA is NVIDIA's open-source Deep Learning Accelerator, partitioned into five sub-blocks (partitions a, c, m, o, p) for hierarchical physical design. Each partition exercises a distinct mix of compute datapath, control, and embedded SRAM macros via FakeRAM, providing multiple medium-to-large accelerator targets from a single upstream design.
